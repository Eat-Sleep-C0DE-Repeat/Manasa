The last 24-hour period has seen the nation record its largest single day spike in COVID-19 cases with 47,704 fresh cases. As per data from the Union Health Ministry, India currently has nearly 5,00,000 active COVID-19 cases, with 9,52,743 discharged, indicating a recovery rate of 64.24 per cent. Despite cities like Mumbai and Delhi, originally the worst hit locations in the country, experiencing remarkable turnarounds over the course of July.
